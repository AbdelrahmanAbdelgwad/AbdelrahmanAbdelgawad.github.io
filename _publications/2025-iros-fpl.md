We introduce Fulfillment Priority Logic (FPL), a logic transforming declarative policy behavior descriptions into utility functions through principled algebraic transformations. Our Balanced Policy Gradient algorithm leverages FPL specifications to achieve up to 500% better sample efficiency compared to Soft Actor-Critic. This is the first implementation of a non-linear utility scalarization design for continuous control.
